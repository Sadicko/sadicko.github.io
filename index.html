01 - About

The person behind
the code

I'm a full-stack software developer and entrepreneur based in Accra, Ghana. I currently serve as a Senior Software Engineer at the Judicial Service of Ghana, where I help build and maintain digital infrastructure for one of Ghana's most critical institutions.

Alongside that, I founded INCREGHANA (registered as INCRESOFT) - a technology company through which I've delivered over 50 projects spanning government portals, legal platforms, fintech products, mobile apps, and AI/ML systems. Everything I've shipped under INCREGHANA, I built personally.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Ghana (graduated Feb. 2026), sharpening the theory behind what I build. My interest in Automated Speech Recognition (ASR) for African languages reflects a deeper conviction: technology should serve the context it lives in.

Beyond the screen, I work in photography and graphic design - because how something looks is as important as how it works.

"I don't just write code - I understand the business problem first, then architect the right solution."
